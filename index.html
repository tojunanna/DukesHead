WELCOME TO THE DUKE'S HEAD IN WISBECH, CAMBRIDGESHIRE.

The Dukes Head is a premium pub, bar, and restaurant, centrally located in the heart of Wisbech, Cambridgeshire.

We features stylish décor and a vibrant atmosphere. This pub is the perfect destination for a delicious lunch, a midweek treat, a three-course meal or celebratory drinks.

Offering a great selection of seasonal dishes and a superb collection of wines and lagers, this family friendly pub, bar, and restaurant will allow you to dine, relax, and mingle in optimum comfort.

With an expansive lunch, dinner and children’s menu, you’ll find a vast selection of freshly prepared, classic pub food dishes that are sure to satisfy event the toughest food critics!

We strive with an outstanding customer services, where our customers comes first.
